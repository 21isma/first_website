Students

  • LIBRARY SERVICES
  • With its extensive electronic resource collection including 130 000+ e-books and 30 000+ printed books, students and academicians are physically served in our central library and reading room. Remote access to e-resources in the library is also available.
  • DINING SERVICES
  • Meals are served at İki Eylül Campus and Porsuk Campus in order to ensure that the students have a healthy and high-quality diet. In addition, social facilities such as canteens, cafeterias, dining halls serve the students.
  • STUDENT CLUBS
  • Student clubs of our university are waiting for you in order to develop individual skills, gain the ability to work in teams, and meet the social and cultural needs of our students. Our university supports our students in joining the clubs they prefer or establishing their own clubs.
  • SPORTS FACILITIES
  • In the university's sports halls, stadium and qualified conference halls with the capacity to organize international olympics and competitions; It hosts panels, congresses and symposiums and offers students a unique campus atmosphere both scientifically and socially.
